Diamond Schmitt’s Mixed-Use Tower

Diamond Schmitt’s Mixed-Use Tower is Canada’s largest urban development, in the form of a 236,000 square foot mixed-use tower in the City of Vaughan, designed by Toronto-based firm Diamond Schmitt Architects and with the collaboration of Rocket Elevators. Situated at the core of SmartCentres Place with easy access to nearby retail and commercial spots, the tower serves as the new home of PwC Canada, in combination with a 100,000-square-foot YMCA, a 20,000-square-foot public library, 10,000-square-foot community space and 3000 square feet of retail. The PwC-YMCA Tower further illustrates the architectural quality and urban design excellence that is a hallmark of this smart, pedestrian-friendly precinct, explained David Larochelle, CEO of Rocket Elevators.
