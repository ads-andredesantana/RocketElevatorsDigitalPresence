The 1335 Howe St

1335 Howe St is another amazing development by Onni with the collaboration of Rocket Elevators. This stunning project consist of 136 luxury homes, situated in the heart of downtown. It is over 40 storey’s and have only 4 or 5 homes per floor. Units in this project will consist of 2- 3- and 4- bedroom homes and range in size from 1,100 – 5,600 square feet. 1335 Howe have spectacular views of both English bay and False Creek and is within walking distance to a huge variety of outstanding bars, restaurants and shops.
